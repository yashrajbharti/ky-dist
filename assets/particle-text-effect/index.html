About Us

Kashiyatra, the Annual Socio-Cultural festival of IIT(BHU) Varanasi is a three-day fiesta, encompassing enchanting literary, musical and artistic events. It is north India's largest cultural festival and takes place within Asia's biggest residential university, BHU. Varanasi, the holy city, houses a plethora of traditions with an ecstatic blend of cultures. IIT(BHU) has been nationally acclaimed for imparting technical knowledge in diverse fields and has recently concluded its centenary celebration, making this edition even more special.
